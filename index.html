    How to Play

    Guess the mystery bird in 5 tries. Type to search, then pick a name from the dropdown — guesses must match a known species.

    Hub & spokes

    The guess box sits at the centre. Around it are 5 clue spokes. You start with just one clue unlocked — the bird song. Every wrong guess unlocks the next spoke.

    Clues unlock in this order:

    1. Bird song — available immediately
    2. Habitat & range
    3. Behaviour & diet
    4. Silhouette — shape & relative size
    5. Full photo — the biggest giveaway, saved for last

    Modes

    Daily Bird — one bird per day, the same for everyone. Progress saves if you close the tab, and results count toward your stats.

    Archive — replay the last 5 daily birds. Great for practice — but Archive rounds don't affect your streak or stats.

    After the game

    Win or lose, a Bird Card reveals the full photo, facts, and the bird's call. On Daily, you can share a spoiler-free result.
