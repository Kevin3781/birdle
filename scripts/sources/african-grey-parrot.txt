# African Grey Parrot (Psittacus erithacus)
# family: Psittacidae  region: africa
# wikipedia: Grey parrot

The grey parrot (Psittacus erithacus), also known as the Congo grey parrot, is an African parrot in the family  Psittacidae. Psittacus erithacus was previously also known as the African grey parrot until the Timneh parrot (Psittacus timneh), previously treated as a subspecies, was elevated to a full species.
