# Common Pochard (Aythya ferina)
# family: Anatidae  region: europe
# wikipedia: Common pochard

The common pochard (; Aythya ferina), known simply as pochard in the United Kingdom, is a medium-sized diving duck in the family Anatidae. It is widespread across the Palearctic. It breeds primarily in Scandinavia and the steppe regions of Siberia, and winters farther south and west.
